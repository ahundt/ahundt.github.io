### [Boost.Container static_vector](is.gd/BoostSV) 
Part of [Boost C++ Libraries](boost.org), `static_vector`, aka `varray`, is a sequence container like `std::vector` with the benefits of statically allocated storage as found in `std::array`. **Acknowledgments**: [Boost.Geometry](is.gd/geometryack), [Boost.Container](is.gd/containerack)
